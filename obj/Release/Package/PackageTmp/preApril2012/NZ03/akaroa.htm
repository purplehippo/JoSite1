THE BANKS PENINSULAR

A quick day trip around the exploded volcano of the Banks Peninsular. Just about managed to stop at most of the Bays on the North side on my way to Akaroa to the East...

At the foot of the foothills ('behind' which is the Peninsular), the capital of Canterbury. Mmmm. See the hustle and bustle!!. Christchurch's Port of Lyttelton. Following the north 'roads' around.
