Work history

I am a developer mainly for web applications, but also analyse business requirements, and write functional specifications. I have eight years experience – four in an airline, three in an insurance company and one for a student travel group. I started writing web applications with Classic ASP, then moved to ASP.NET. My experience includes multi-tier applications, with business objects, data access layers, the front end and web services; I also have responsibility for database design and test data.

I have worked independently for complete development life-cycles – from gathering requirements, through development, to test, integration and user support. More recently, I have been a member of a team working on all tiers of a large .NET web project; also included gathering requirements for a functional specification

The work included both hardware design and test, and creating (organising) a components database with Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Access.

I am a cheerful, bubbly person with a good eye for detail. I work well independently and in a team, and interact well with many types of people. My eagerness to be involved and ability to communicate well is reflected by my directorship and committee posts.
